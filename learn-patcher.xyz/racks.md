# Racks

## Create a rack

* navigate to “collection”
* click “Create Rack”
* Open your new rack

## Add modules to a rack

* Create a rack
* navigate to “modules”
* Search for a module, open it
* Click on the ‘add to rack’
* Confirm

## Edit a rack

* Open rack
* Click on the little lock button to lock/unlock
* Drag and drop to move modules in rack
* Save is automatic
